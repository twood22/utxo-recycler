System Documentation

What is a dust UTXO?

A dust UTXO is a very small Bitcoin output that costs more in fees to spend than it's worth. These accumulate over time and bloat the Bitcoin blockchain's UTXO set.

Why {{ payout_percent }}% payout?

We incentivize consolidation of dust UTXOs to reduce Bitcoin's UTXO set size, making the network more efficient for everyone.

What wallets work?

Any wallet supporting Lightning addresses (LNURL-pay): Alby, Wallet of Satoshi, Phoenix, Zeus, Breez, and others.
